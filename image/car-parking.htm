Car Parking

Parking at Hotel Grand Chancellor Brisbane

The hotel has secure, undercover car parking available, including 2 charging bays for Tesla electric vehicles.

The car park is accessible 24 hours per day for in-house guests and a ticket provides multiple entries and exits during your stay.

Hotel Guest 

$30 per day (7 days per week) 

 

Conference Guest 

$20 per day 

 

Casual Parking 

0 – 4 hours $15 

4 hours and above $35
